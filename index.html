Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hats

How do I find my hat size?

Use a measuring tape to measure around your head where the hat will sit. Check our size guide for detailed instructions and measurements.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn items in their original packaging. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

How do I care for my hat?

Each hat comes with specific care instructions. Generally, store in a cool, dry place and clean according to the material type.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.
